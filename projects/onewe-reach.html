Designing a full-day experiential health summit and post-event digital platform for healthcare professionals — from persona architecture to product launch.

The Problem

Healthcare professionals know the science. They weren't using it on themselves.

One We Reach was designing a full-day peak performance summit for professionals working across healthcare. The challenge was getting them to see themselves as the patient — to apply the same evidence-based scrutiny they use at work to their own healthspan.

"How do you design an experience that makes a VP of Commercial Strategy or a hospitalist physician stop performing expertise — and actually look in the mirror?"

How I Think

The process behind the persona system.

1

Anchor each persona to a disease area and a performance identity

Rather than building generic health archetypes, I anchored each of the 10 personas to a specific disease area and a professional identity that would resonate with the room — so every attendee could find themselves in at least one persona.

2

Integrate validated science, not invented data

Every persona includes a McCance Brain Care Score and a Health Literacy Score. I selected the Stilley instrument for clinical validity and secured formal permission from the University of Pittsburgh authors to use it in the summit context.

3

Design for the third-person effect, then the mirror

Participants spend the morning inhabiting someone else's health story, then at the 'taking off the mask' moment, turn that same lens on themselves. The third-person distance is what makes the self-reflection land.

4

Write prompts that provoke, not instruct

Each persona includes journey reflections — hypothetical life events designed to surface the gaps between knowing and doing. That gap is exactly what the summit exists to close.

The Digital Platform

The summit was one day. The platform is what makes it last.

I conceived and managed the development of a post-summit platform to sustain the behavior change the summit starts. I defined every feature, wrote the product requirements, and managed the developer through build and iteration.

Brain Care Score tracker

Attendees log and track their personal BCS across 30, 60, and 90-day milestones.

90-day action plan builder

Structured tool for building a personalized commitment plan from the summit day exercise.

Community feed

Peer accountability space where attendees share reflections and progress with cohort members.

Persona library

All 10 summit personas accessible post-event — so attendees can revisit and share.

Resource hub

Curated frameworks and disease-area content organized by the summit's clinical themes.

Cohort dashboard

Facilitator-facing view of aggregate BCS trends and community activity.
